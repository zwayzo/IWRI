Global Context & challenges

Population growth

Population growth, urbanization along with ongoing developments in economic activities related to industry, agriculture and tourism will increase pressure on water availability across the glob

Global models

Global models predict an increase in the intensity and frequency of extreme events such as hot temperatures, floods, and droughts with persistent tendency of reduction of water availability in most parts of the world

Despite water

Despite water availability in some parts of Africa, it is not evenly distributed, this continent is not yet benefiting in an optimized way from its water resources mainly for urban and industrial supply, agriculture and hydropower.
